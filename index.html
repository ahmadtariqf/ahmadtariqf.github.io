Biography

I am a data scientist and AI engineer with a strong research background in symbolic AI, machine learning, and medical imaging. My goal is to build intelligent systems that are trustworthy, explainable, and beneficial to society.

Research Interests

I am passionate about creating transparent and interpretable AI systems that combine symbolic reasoning with statistical learning. My work includes:

  • Applying rough set theory for discretization and feature selection in complex datasets
  • Designing deep learning architectures for medical image segmentation, including 2D/3D MRI brain scans
  • Developing symbolic models for reasoning under uncertainty using logic-based frameworks
  • Building AI-powered platforms to support social welfare, using real-world data from government institutions
  • Exploring ethical AI applications that enhance transparency and trust in automated decision-making

I believe in bridging the gap between theoretical AI research and practical societal impact.

Background

I hold a Master’s degree in Data Science from Princess Sumaya University for Technology and a Bachelor’s in Software Engineering from Al-Hussein Bin Talal University, where I graduated first in my class. I currently work at the National Aid Fund in Jordan as a researcher and data engineer, developing and maintaining intelligent social protection data systems.
